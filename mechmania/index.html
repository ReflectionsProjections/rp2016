What is Mechmania?


MechMania is a 24 hour Game AI hackathon. It’s a part of ACM@Illinois’s Reflections | Projections Conference. We’ll unveil at the MM opening. You will have 24 hours to develop an AI to play this game. A tournament at the end will determine the best AIs. The teams that made the best AI’s get awesome prizes ;)

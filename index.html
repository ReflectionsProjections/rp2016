What is Reflections|Projections?

Reflections | Projections 2016 is the 22nd iteration of a tech conference organized and run by students at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. We draw students, speakers, and companies from around the world. Join us to listen to tech talks from across the industry, to participate in an Artificial Intelligence programming competition in Mechmania, to solve puzzles throughout the conference during PuzzleBang!, and to attend our job and startup fairs.

If you wish to be involved with any parts of the conference, send an email to conference-chair@acm.illinois.edu
